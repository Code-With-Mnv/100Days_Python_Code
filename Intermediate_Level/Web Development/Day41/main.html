Best webseries to watch in 2024.

These are my top 3 favourite webseries!

> ASUR: WELCOME TO YOUR DARK SIDE (PART 1 / 2)

Asur is an Indian psychological thriller web series that blends mythology with modern crime investigation.
The story follows two forensic experts, Nikhil and Dhananjay, who are drawn into a cat-and-mouse game with a mysterious serial killer inspired by ancient Indian myths.
The show delves into the dark and twisted minds of both the criminals and those trying to catch them, creating a suspenseful and gripping narrative that explores the battle between good and evil.

> PANCHAYAT (PART 1 / 2 / 3)

Panchayat is an Indian web series that follows the life of Abhishek, an engineering graduate who reluctantly takes up a job as a Panchayat secretary in a remote village named Phulera.
The show humorously depicts the challenges and quirks of rural life as Abhishek navigates his responsibilities while dealing with the eccentric villagers.
It blends comedy and drama, offering a heartwarming and realistic portrayal of rural India.

> SPECIAL OPS (PART 1 / 1.5)
